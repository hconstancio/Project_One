The World Cup Athelete
World cup national teams are composed of players that spend most of their time and game minutes
playing for a variety of clubs in different countries and leagues of different quality.

Players can play in 40 league games in a season depending on the size of the league they play in.
This provides a much larger sample size of to measure individual performances than the limited international schedule.

For my analysis I wanted to discover if the dominance of the players league performances aggregated
to their international clubs related to the success of the international clubs in the World Cup.

There was not much data available from API's. As it turns out, no one's investing much in distrubiting
the results of the Cypriot First division soccer league.
